# Contributor License Note

This project is intended to be available under dual licensing:

- AGPL-3.0-or-later for open-source use
- a separate commercial license from the copyright holder for organizations that need different terms

## Why this note exists

Dual licensing becomes legally complicated when many external contributors hold copyright in different parts of the codebase.
To keep licensing manageable, maintainers may require additional confirmation for non-trivial future contributions.

## Current expectation

By contributing code, documentation, or other material to this repository, you confirm that:

1. you have the right to submit the contribution
2. the contribution is your own work or you are legally allowed to submit it
3. the project maintainers may distribute that contribution under the repository's open-source license terms

## Future contributor agreement

For larger future contributions, maintainers may introduce a dedicated Contributor License Agreement (CLA) or copyright assignment workflow.
If that happens, contributors may be asked to explicitly sign or confirm those terms before the contribution is accepted.

This file is a practical project note and not a substitute for legal advice.
